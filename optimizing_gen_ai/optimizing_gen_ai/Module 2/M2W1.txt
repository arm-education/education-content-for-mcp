Welcome to this module on Generative AI
Models.

Over the past few years,
transformers have reshaped the landscape

of artificial intelligence,
powering everything from chat bots to

image generators.

But what exactly makes transformers so
powerful,

and how do they become large language
models like ChatGPT?

In this session,
we'll take a deep dive into the

architecture that underpins modern AI.

You'll learn how key innovations—like
residual connections, self attention,

and positional encodings—allow
transformers to model complex patterns

across language, vision, and more.

We'll then look at how a basic
transformer becomes a full-fledged LLM.

From massive self-supervised pre-training
to fine-tuning with human-labeled data

and reinforcement learning,
you'll get a complete picture of the

training pipeline.

Finally,
we'll explore how inference works—

how models generate responses in real
time, process prompts,

and use KV caching to do it all
efficiently.

By the end of this module,
you'll understand the structure, training,

and inference strategies behind the most
influential AI models of our time.
